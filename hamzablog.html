Mapping Change — My Geo‑Informatics Portfolio

I turn spatial data into decisions. From national land‑cover mapping to real‑time water dashboards and drone-based post‑disaster assessments — here are projects that matter.

By Hamza Nkhumbwa • Nov 5, 2025

SBAPP — National Land Cover Mapping

Spatial Biodiversity Assessment, Prioritization and Planning (SBAPP) — collaborative national mapping using Google Earth Engine to support conservation and policy.

Golomoti — Topographic Survey

Field surveying in Golomoti: GNSS collection, precision site maps, and satellite basemaps combined to support land management and planning at the community level.

Explore Golomoti Field notes

Urban Planning — Lilongwe City Council

Digitizing land records and supporting the UN Urban Resilience Plan — using ArcGIS Pro, QGIS and drone imagery to inform urban growth and site layouts.

Read urban planning story Impact summary

AgriSight — Smart Farming Dashboard

A drone + GIS dashboard built to monitor soil fertility and crop health — designed to help farmers make fast, data-driven decisions to improve yields sustainably.

Open AgriSight Key features

NWRA — Groundwater & Water Dashboard

Prototype dashboard for National Water Resources Authority: permit tracking, basin delineation, and visualization for groundwater management and policy oversight.

View NWRA dashboard sample Why this helps

Dzalanyama Forest — Land Use Change Prediction

Machine Learning‑based prediction of land use and cover change for Dzalanyama Forest. Integrates historical data and models to forecast future environmental impacts.

Post‑Disaster Mapping — Ngabu Floods

Volunteer drone missions to map flood extents and support emergency response in Chikwawa District — high-resolution maps for humanitarian action.

Short takeaways

  • Integrate field collection with satellite & drone analytics to increase data accuracy.
