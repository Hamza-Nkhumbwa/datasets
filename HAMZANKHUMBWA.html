Drone Technology President

Responsible for pre-flight and post-flight safety checks, mission planning, and supervising UAV operations — including drone airworthiness, battery management, and safe deployment during missions.

PRE-FLIGHT / POST-FLIGHT CHECKLIST
  • Check battery charge level and secure battery compartment
  • Inspect propellers for damage and ensure correct mounting
  • Verify gimbal covers removed and gimbal stabilised
  • Confirm firmware and software versions are up-to-date
  • Calibrate compass and IMU if required
  • Verify GPS lock and telemetry link
  • Check sensors and cameras for cleanliness and functionality
  • Secure payload (if any) and confirm mounting points
  • Post-flight: inspect for damage, log flight time and battery cycles
