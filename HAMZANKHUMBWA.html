State of the Map Malawi 2025

As an active contributor to the OpenStreetMap community, I'm proud to be part of organizing State of the Map Malawi 2025 - the premier gathering for open mapping enthusiasts, GIS professionals, and community mappers across Malawi.

This initiative brings together stakeholders from government, NGOs, academia, and the private sector to share knowledge, build capacity, and advance the use of open geospatial data for national development.
